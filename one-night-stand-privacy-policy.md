# Privacy Policy
**One Night Stand**
_Last Updated: 2025-12-27_

One Night Stand (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a consumer social product owned and operated by **Match Mate Group (Consumer Social Products)** and developed by **Quantum Times Technologies**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data when you use our website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

By using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Eligibility
The Services are strictly for users **18 years and above**. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

- Profile data (photos, bio, gender, preferences)
- Communications with other users or support
- Payment and subscription information (if applicable)

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically
- Device identifiers, IP address, browser type
- Usage data (swipes, matches, interactions)

### 3.3 Information from Third Parties

- Analytics and advertising partners

We use personal data to:

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Services and comply with legal obligations. Deleted accounts are anonymized or permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe.
